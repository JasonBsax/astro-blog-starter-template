One of the greatest insights that I learned in this course on digital entrepreneurship is the enormous impact that media can make on whether or not a company succeeds. In an interconnected society, having a good product or service is not enough; what matters now is whether people know about your business and whether they are interested in it.

Online marketing and social media have come to represent absolute cornerstones for any company in any niche and of any size. Regardless of whether you’re just starting out as an individual businessperson working on a side hustle, or running a huge international business empire, your online presence affects the way people will view you and will influence their decisions on whether they should give you their business. Not having an online presence now means being practically nonexistent, just like putting up a shop in a deserted spot.

In particular, social media platforms have brought about an entirely new paradigm when it comes to business interactions. Where large sums of money were previously necessary to get one’s point across, a simple blog post or short video will suffice today. Social media platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X provide businesses with an opportunity to reach out directly to millions of individuals while fostering an environment in which communities can be formed.

In addition to social networking sites, maintaining an effective online presence overall, such as maintaining a professional website, achieving good search engine ranking, utilizing email marketing, and producing content consistently, helps develop the trust required for converting visitors into customers.

Through this class, we learned that there is no other way out than to be digitally literate for any entrepreneur today. Knowing how to effectively utilize media is one of the crucial skills that a businessman needs to acquire and something I will take even after graduating from the classroom.
